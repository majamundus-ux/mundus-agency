The Challenge

Hiring is breaking your focus.

At the Series A/B stage, recruiting becomes one of the most time-consuming parts of running a company — and the most expensive to get wrong.

01

You're doing it yourself

Sourcing, screening, scheduling, debriefing — it's eating 10–15 hours of your week. That's time that should be going toward product, customers, and investors.

02

Agencies aren't aligned with you

Traditional recruiters are paid per placement. They're incentivized to fill seats, not find the right people. You end up with volume — not quality.

03

An in-house hire is premature

A full-time recruiter costs $90K–$130K/year before benefits. At the Series A stage, that's a significant overhead bet — and you'd still be managing them daily.

How It Works

We become part of your team.

Mundus doesn't operate at arm's length. We integrate into your process, learn your culture, and act as your internal recruiting function — at a fraction of the cost.

1

Discovery & Alignment

We start with a structured onboarding session to understand your company, culture, team dynamics, and what "the right hire" actually means for each role. This is what separates a partner from a vendor.

2

Sourcing & Outreach

We build targeted pipelines on LinkedIn, GitHub, industry networks, and our proprietary database. Every outreach message is personalized — not templated spam.

3

Screening & Vetting

Every candidate goes through a structured screening process: initial screen, skills assessment, culture fit evaluation, and a detailed scorecard — before you ever see their name.

4

Shortlist & Coordination

You receive a curated shortlist of 3–5 fully vetted candidates per role — with our written assessment of each. We coordinate all scheduling, feedback loops, and debrief sessions.

5

Offer & Closing Support

We support offer structuring, negotiation, and candidate communication through to acceptance — and stay in contact through the first 30 days to ensure a strong start.
