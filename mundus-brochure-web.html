The 4 rules

What keeps candidates
in your process.

Apply these starting with your next open role.

01
Respond within 24 hours. Every time.
Top candidates run parallel processes. Silence reads as disinterest — even when it isn't. Set a rule: no candidate waits more than 24 hours for a response.
02
Define the process upfront.
On the first call, tell them exactly what happens next — and when. Candidates who know the timeline stay in it. Candidates left guessing start looking elsewhere.
03
Keep them warm between steps.
"We're still on track, next step is Thursday" takes 30 seconds. That message is the difference between a candidate who waits and one who moves on.
04
Sell the vision before the offer.
By the time you make an offer, they should already want to say yes. Every touchpoint is a chance to make the opportunity feel real — not just the final conversation.
